Recipe #1

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2 people
Ingredients Quantity
|Bread & Cheese| |2 slices and as much cheese as you want!|

Step 1: Get Your Bread. Step 2:Put your stove on Medium-Low heat Step 3:Place your slightly buttered peice of bread on a pan and place it on your stove Step 4:Put cheese on your bread and then put another piece of bread on top of that. Step 5: Wait a few minutes for the cheese to melt,flip it, and your done!!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
|Pasta & Red Sauce| |A full box of Penne Pasta and a jar of Red Sauce|

Step 1: Boil your water with a pinch of salt. Step 2:When your water is bubbling, add your pasta. Step 3:Cook your Pasta to Al Dente. (Soft but still chewy) Step 4:When done cooking, drain your pasta and plce in a bowl Step 5:Add your red sauce and your done!! Extra:Heat up some ground beet and add it to your sauce for a little extra taste.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
|Pasta, Milk, Cheese| |Cup of Pasta, 3 tablespoons of Milk, as much Cheese as you want.|

Step 1: Put your pasta and water (should be the same amount for both) with a pinch of salt in a cup. Step 2:Microwave for 3 minutes on high. Step 3:Take it out and add three tablespoons of milk and lots of cheese. Step 4:Put it in the microwave again until creamy and gooey. Step 5:Stir and enjoy!! Extra:Add breadcrumbs on the top for a nice crunch!!
